Titan

"What does it mean to be a Titan? As a Titan, you are a part of the City - in a way no Warlock or Hunter could understand. The dream of the City rests upon our shoulders." â€” Commander Zavala They are a class of Guardians, that have the strongest armour, defences and offense out of all 3, essentially playing the role of a tank in the fireteam.

Overview

They have armour that is equipped with power that boosts their strength and endurance. Titans utilize this strenth to completely decimate their enemies.
Titans have been honing the light of the Traveler for centuries, it is due to this power that they were able to produce the City, taking a blood oath to make sure it always remains strong.

Abilities

Every guardian class has their own special ability set, given by the Traveler, which is unique from other classes, although some are shared between them such as, "Double jump", and "Grenade-based" abilities whose effects vary by class. However, Titans have special sets that no other class has with firepower unparalled among them.

Subclasses

>> Defender:

"The wall against which the Darkness breaks." A Void subclass that shields and buffs the overall damage of the fireteam.

Supers:

- Ward of Dawn:

Summons a small bubble-like shield around the player, which protects and empoweres weapon damage for a short period of time.

>> Sunbreaker:

"Forge the fury of undying suns." A Solar artillery subclass with strong focus in offense, recovery, and ability buffs.

Supers:

- Hammer of Sol:

Summons a flaming hammer, wrecking havoc among the battlefield.

- Burning Maul:

Summons a flaming cudgel, allowing players to slam it on the ground to create bursts of flame.

>> Striker:

"At close quarters a fist is better than any gun." An Arc combat based subclass with emphasis on crowd-control, heavy damage and armour.

Supers:

- Thundercrash:

Launches the player towards their desired target, dealing massive amounts of burst damage.

- Fist of Havoc:

An area-of-effect ground punches, good for crowd-control.
