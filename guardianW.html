Warlock

Warlocks are a class of guardians that utilise powers given by the Traveler for both offense and defence. Warlocks specialize in ranged melee and cast spells to finish enemies. They are also excellent support classes as they can heal and give a significant enhancement buff to weapons for damage.

Overview

Warlocks were the first to obtain the power of the traveler, wielding it's Light, they devoted themselves to study the Traveler, uncovering its mysterious appeareance and the power it has brought with it, learning new ways to harness it and use it for the betterment of humanity.

Abilities

Every guardian class has their own special ability set, given by the Traveler, which is unique from other classes, although some are shared between them such as, "Double jump", and "Grenade-based" abilities whose effects vary by class. However, here are the abilities of each subclass in Warlocks.

Subclasses

>> Voidwalker:

"From the smallest atoms bloom the greatest explosions." A damage based Void subclass with emphasis on recovering health and ability energy.

Supers:

- Nova Bomb:

When used, the player leaps into the air and unleashes a large void blast from their hands.

- Nova Warp:

Teleport around the field and unleash explosions along ur path to make enemies volatile.

>> Sunsinger:

"There are flames that even the Darkness cannot extinguish." A support-focused Solar subclass with emphasis on buffing you and your fireteam.

Supers:

- Radiance:

This super ability gives an overpowered buff to you and your allies, creating an aura, which increases weapon damage, survivability and teamplay.

>> Stormcaller:

"Harmony within, hurricane without." A crowd-control subclass fueled by Arc light.

Supers:

- Stormtrance:

Chaining Arc lightning from the player's hands, efficient in crowd control and semi-efficient in boss damage.

- Chaos Reach:

Unleash a beam of Arc Lightning from your hands, very efficient in boss damage.
