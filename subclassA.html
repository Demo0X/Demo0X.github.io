Arc Light is related to electricity and electromagnetism, it is wielded by guardians to generate lightning bolts and electromagnetic phenomena. Most of Golden Age technology that Humanity have possesses circuitry, generators, and holograms. Arc is also found in weather systems as well as other enemy types that harness it such as Screebs or Abominations.

How Guardians Wield Arc

Warlocks

Warlocks use arc in a more offensive way by projecting Arc light into a singular beam or harnessing the power of lightning itself for major room clears against a number of enemies.

Hunters

Hunters create an electrified bo-staff known as Arc Staff, using it to clear large groups of enemies with martial arts or throwing it on a larger enemy to deal a good amount of damage with lingering effects as well.

Titans

Titans have the most offensive power when it comes to Arc, as they have a super where they superman launch themselves into a singular target dealing large amount of single target burst damage.

Arc Fragments

Arc Fragments in Destiny are known to be one of the best for dense number of enemies due to the sheer amount of buffs and effects you can obtain due to it, some of which being Jolt as well as Blinding effects where you can blind enemies to stay safe in an area or Jolt for single target destruction by directly imbuing Arc light on to them.
