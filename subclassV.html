Overview

"Did you know it used to be controversial to harness the Void? Maybe one day the Vanguard can move past the Light entirely. I'm not holdin' my breath." - The Drifter
Void energy is related to dark/energy matter and gravitational attraction, it is wielded by Guardians to produce black holes, spatial warps, and other distortions of space. It was once considered taboo to use Void powers, as it was viewed as "dark and evil", however, void itself is defined as being absent of Light or Darkness and Void Light is just the usage of it.

Void-based Races

Fallen Servitors

The Fallen, also known as Eliksni, are a type of insectoid alien species, notorious for their raids and scavenges. Fallen Servitors are the only type of creatures within their race that show void energy by their blasts, alongside the substantial artillery of Heavy Shanks, and Brigs.

Cabal

The Cabal are one of the leading main alien races in D2. War and Glory are the only things the Cabal value, where the strongest soldiers are at the top of the hiearchy in their military standings. The Cabal have adopted Bronto Cannons and one of their weaker soldiers, the Psions, utilize mainly Void abilities that launch players into the air.
