Subclass

Overview

"Did you know it used to be controversial to harness the Void? Maybe one day the Vanguard can move past the Light entirely. I'm not holdin' my breath." - The Drifter
Void energy is related to dark/energy matter and gravitational attraction, it is wielded by Guardians to produce black holes, spatial warps, and other distortions of space. It was once considered taboo to use Void powers, as it was viewed as "dark and evil", however, void itself is defined as being absent of Light or Darkness and Void Light is just the usage of it.

How Guardians Wield Void

Warlocks

Warlocks harness the void by unleashing a black hole in the form of a medium-sized ball called the nova bomb that disintegrates nearby enemies into oblivion.

Hunters

Hunters use Void lights weakening abilities by shooting 1 or 3 shots from their bows that tether enemies, debuffing them and mark them all together, sharing damage.

Titans

Titans have utilized Void in a more defensive manner where they create a bubble to protect nearby allies and themselves by providing an overshield that keeps players safe from damage.

Void Fragments

Void Fragments in Destiny are one of the most overpowered in the entire game as they can provide a number of buffs and effects that no other class can give such as Devour, or Overcharged grenades both of which are very efficient in end-level content, Devour giving insant healing on Orb of Power pickups and Overcharged grenades boosting grenade damage and reducing cooldowns significantly if paired with exotics.
